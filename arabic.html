About the course

Arabic is the 4th most commonly spoken language in the world with over 295 million speakers. Learn Arabic with Duolingo to communicate with locals when you travel to Egypt, Morocco, Jordan, Lebanon or any of the 27 countries where Arabic is an official language. Use your new skills to appreciate beautiful Arabic calligraphy and literature or flawlessly order shawarma in Cairo!
